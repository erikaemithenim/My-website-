📖 Company Story

Founded in 2021, NeuralForge AI began as a research project at Stanford University's AI Lab. Co-founders Arjun Kapoor and Sarah Rodriguez envisioned a world where every enterprise could harness the power of advanced neural networks without needing a team of PhDs.

Today, NeuralForge serves 200+ enterprise clients across 14 countries, providing no-code AI solutions that automate complex business processes. Their proprietary "ForgeNet" architecture achieves state-of-the-art performance while being 10x more efficient than competing solutions.

With partnerships with Microsoft, AWS, and SAP, NeuralForge is positioned to become the definitive AI infrastructure layer for the enterprise world. Their recent Series B round was oversubscribed by 3x, signaling massive investor confidence.
